About the Project

Designed and delivered a custom CRM software solution forsmall to large-sized construction firms struggling to manage client relationships, leads, and project communications. The platform enabled sales automation, streamlined lead-to-contract workflows, and centralized client data, boosting operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Implemented in a fast-paced Agile-Scrum environment, the project emphasized incremental delivery, rapid feedback loops, and adaptability to evolving client needs.The system integrated with cloud telephony, WhatsApp, SMS, email, and Mailchimp.
