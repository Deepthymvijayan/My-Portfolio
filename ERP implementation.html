Helped a mid sized construction company to run their business efficiently by integrating business process.

About the Project

The mid-sized construction firm faced challenges in managing procurement, finance, and HR processes efficiently. Their manual systems led to frequent errors and delays in financial reporting, hindering smooth business operations and decision-making.

Project Duration: 45 Days

Scope: Procurement, Finance, and HR Modules

Role: Project Coordinator

Team Size: 12 (Developers, Consultants, QA, DevOps, Executives)

Responsibilities and Impact

Responsibilities:

  • Defined the project scope, timeline, and resource allocation during the Project Preparation phase.
  • Coordinated ERP configuration and minimized unnecessary customizations during the Realization phase.
  • Managed unit testing, integration testing, and User Acceptance Testing (UAT), ensuring all business processes were validated.
  • Directed Final Preparation and Go-Live activities, ensuring smooth transition and minimal disruptions.

Results & Business Impact:

  • Procurement Errors: Reduced procurement errors by 30%, optimizing vendor management and improving the overall procurement process.
  • Financial Reporting: Enhanced financial reporting accuracy by 50%, reducing month-end closing time from 10 to 5 days, enabling quicker financial decisions.
  • Post-Launch Stability: Achieved zero major post-launch issues, ensuring a seamless transition to the new system without business disruptions.

Project Management Impact:

  • Change Management: Mitigated resistance through structured stakeholder engagement and training programs.
  • Team Collaboration: Facilitated cross-functional collaboration using Agile ceremonies, ensuring alignment and continuous feedback.
  • Servant Leadership: Empowered team members to take ownership of their tasks and resolve challenges autonomously.
  • Agile Implementation: Delivered incremental value with regular feedback loops, adapting to evolving business requirements.
  • Stakeholder Benefits: Ensured a smooth transition with minimal downtime, resulting in high user satisfaction.
  • Ethics & Transparency: Maintained open communication with stakeholders, ensuring ethical decision-making and data integrity.
  • Cultural & Organizational Adaptability: Promoted an inclusive work environment, recognizing and respecting diverse perspectives and working styles.

Methodologies & Tools

  • Project Management: Agile, Waterfall, Hybrid
  • Tools: Jira, MS Teams,ERP, SQL, Power BI
  • Testing: Unit Testing, Integration Testing, UAT, Performance Testing
  • Security & Data Migration: Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), Master Data Validation
